The Land Of Honey

2022

The land of honey is a virtual reality 3D experience inspired from the The City of Lost Children is a 1995
The story takes place in a steamy, magical, burning hot desert city, where a young girl is stuck into her dream and reality. Using the light trees and the wind chime sounds as leading clues to find a way to escape this puzzle. She needs to find the fortune cookie and the ultimate tree of freedom to turn her nightmares down.

Tools: Unity, Blender, Premiere Pro, QuickTime
Editing & Modeling & Design: Yulai Fan

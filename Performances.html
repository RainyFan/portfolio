Let me sleepwalk through this city


2022

The performance “Let Me Sleepwalk Through This City” (2022) responds to the disorienting experience of navigating post-quarantine public spaces in NYC. The movement and sound draw on the non-neutrality of public spaces conditioned by patriarchal capitalism and recently, increasing racial tension and violence. The visual and aural elements draw on the sensation of riding the NYC subway: disjunctive sounds, disruptive visual cues and texts, bodily smells, and brief, close encounters with stranger-passers-by. Referencing everyday pedestrian movement, the performance unpacks the physiological responses to environmental signals not particular to but heightened in the transit system of this city.

Luminous Hues Grow on My Dancing Body

2021

Luminous Hues Grow on My Dancing Body" is a performance art that combines lighting design and dance. It explores the intimate interaction between the human body and luminaire —— the physical touch of the human body on the luminaire creates various colors that print back on the human. During such interaction, the human body and the luminaire are no longer separate beings but become a symbiotic whole.

Performance: Yulai Fan, Denzil Zhang
